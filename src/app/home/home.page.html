About

The Journal of Management Research and Development (JMRD) is a quarterly publication of the Department of Business Administration, Faculty of Administration, Nasarawa State University, Keffi. The JMRD focuses on advancing the knowledge and practice of management in various fields. The journal publishes original research articles, reviews, and case studies that explore emerging trends, best practices, and innovative solutions in management. It provides a platform for academics, researchers, and practitioners to share their insights, theories, and empirical findings, and encourages interdisciplinary approaches to problem-solving.

The Journal of Management Research and Development (JMRD) also aims to promote the dissemination of knowledge and the application of management principles in various sectors. It covers topics such as strategic management, organizational behavior, human resource management, entrepreneurship, innovation, marketing, finance, operations management, and related fields. The JMRD targets a wide range of readers, including researchers, educators, students, policy-makers, and practitioners who seek to enhance their understanding and skills in management. Overall, the JMRD serves as a valuable resource for advancing management theory and practice, and for shaping the future of management.
